Whitney Houston

Life

Whitney Houston is The daughter of singer Cissy Houston. She was born in Westfield, NJ. She started singing at the age of 11 at her mothers Baptist church and went on to be a backup vocalist for musicians such as Chaka Khan, Dionne Warwick and Michael Zagar.

Fame

Houston shot to fame after signing her first record deal with Arista Records and releasing her self-titled album Whitney Houston. Houston's first acting role was as the star of the feature film The Bodyguard (1992). The film's original soundtrack won a Grammy Award in 1994 for Album of the Year and remains the best-selling single by a woman in music history.

Last moments

Houston was found dead in her hotel room at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles on February 11, 2012. The cause of her death was initially thought to be accidental drowning. Leter on the coroner concluded that while drowing was the main cause of death, it was heart problems and years of drug use that ultimately lead to Houston's death.
